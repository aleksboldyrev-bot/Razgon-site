📮 Contact

For bugs, questions, or feature requests, email hello@razgon.app. You'll hear back from the developer — there's no support tier to climb.

🛌 My readiness score says "pending" or seems off

Readiness needs last night's sleep from Apple Health. If your Apple Watch is passcode-locked, sleep won't sync until it's unlocked — enable Watch → Passcode → "Unlock with iPhone" to make this automatic. Scores personalize over your first ~2 weeks as your baseline builds.

🍎 Health data isn't showing

Check iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Razgon and make sure the data types you want are enabled. Razgon only reads what you allow.

🔍 Food search or barcode returns nothing

Lookups use the USDA and Open Food Facts databases and need an internet connection. Some regional products aren't in the databases yet — you can add any food manually and reuse it later.

🗑️ How do I delete my data?

Your logs live only on your device: deleting the app deletes them. Health data written to Apple Health can be reviewed and removed in the Health app.
